My name is John Harden and I'm a journalist based in Texas working for the 2015 Pulitzer Prize winning newspaper the Houston Chronicle.


As a data journalist my job is to take heavy data sets and turn them into stories that the average reader won't glaze over. I started working for the Chronicle in 2014. Before that I worked in Dallas-Fort Worth for the A.H. Belo owned newspaper The Denton Record-Chronicle.


It was there where I won two investigative awards -- one from the Texas Associated Press Managing Editors and the second from the Society of Professional Journalists.

At the Houston Chronicle my work and interactives have been honored and recognized by the Texas Press Managing Editors and the National Press Foundation.

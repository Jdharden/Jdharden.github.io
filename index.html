ABOUT ME

Database Journalist

As a data journalist my job is to take heavy data sets, analyze them and turn them into narratives that shows the people behind the numbers. I started working for the Post in Summer 2018 after leaving the Houston Chronicle. In 2018, I was a part of the team recognized as a Pulitzer Prize finalist for its coverage of Hurricane Harvey in fall 2017.
